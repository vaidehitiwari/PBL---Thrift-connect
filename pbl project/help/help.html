Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find specific thrift items?

You can browse by category.

Are all the Instagram sellers verified?

Yes, we verify each seller on Instagram to ensure authenticity and trustworthiness.

Can I suggest a store?

Absolutely! Reach out to us through our contact form to suggest any store you believe should be featured.

Do you handle payments for purchases?

No, Thrift Konnect is a directory that connects you to sellers; payments and are handled directly by the Instagram stores.

Can I return an item or cancel an order?

Returns, exchanges, or cancellations depend on the individual seller's policies. Please contact them directly for return and exchange information.

How do I report a seller?

If you encounter any issues with a seller, please let us know by reaching out through our contact page.

Shipping & Returns

We do not handle shipping directly, as Thrift Konnect connects you with Instagram sellers. Please refer to individual seller policies for shipping and returns.
